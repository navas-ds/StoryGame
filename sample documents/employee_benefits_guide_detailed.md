# Employee Benefits Guide

## Overview

This guide outlines the benefits available to full-time employees. Some
benefits begin immediately, while others are available after successful
completion of the probation period.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Health & Medical Benefits

### Health Insurance

-   Company-sponsored health insurance after probation.
-   Covers hospitalization, emergency treatment, and day-care
    procedures.
-   Cashless treatment at network hospitals.
-   Annual preventive health check-up.

### Dental & Vision

-   Dental reimbursement up to ₹5,000 per year.
-   Vision allowance for prescription glasses every two years.

### Mental Wellness

-   Confidential counselling sessions.
-   Employee Assistance Program (EAP).
-   Stress management workshops.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Financial Benefits

### Annual Bonus

Performance-based bonus reviewed every December.

### Provident Fund

Statutory PF contributions as per government regulations.

### Gratuity

Eligible after five years of continuous service.

### Group Life Insurance

Company-sponsored life insurance coverage.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Learning & Development

### Annual Learning Budget

Each employee receives **₹25,000 per year** for: - Online courses -
Certifications - Technical books - Conferences - Workshops

Manager approval is required before reimbursement.

### Internal Training

-   Technical sessions
-   Leadership programs
-   Communication workshops
-   AI and Cloud training
